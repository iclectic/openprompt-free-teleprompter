Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 20, 2026

OpenPrompt ("we", "our", "the app") is a free teleprompter application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Local Data (stored on your device only):

  • Scripts you create (title, content, tags)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Onboarding completion status

This data is stored in your device's local storage and is never uploaded to our servers.

Authentication Data (optional):

  • If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google via Firebase Authentication.
  • This information is used solely to identify your account and is processed by Google Firebase.
  • You can use the app as a guest without providing any personal information.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not read, upload, or share your script content.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect device identifiers for tracking purposes.
  • We do not sell any data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — for optional sign-in functionality. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. Camera Access

The app requests camera access only when you use Record Mode (camera + teleprompter overlay). Camera data is displayed locally in real-time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted by the app.

5. Microphone Access

The app requests microphone access only when you enable voice control in the teleprompter player. Audio is processed locally on your device using the Web Speech API and is never recorded or transmitted by the app.

6. Children's Privacy

OpenPrompt is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local data: Deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Account data: You can sign out at any time from Settings. To delete your Firebase account data, contact us at the email below.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use the app without creating an account (guest mode).
  • Export all your scripts as a backup file.
  • Delete all your local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling.
  • Request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective date" above.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

Cuevora ("we", "our", "us", or "the app") is a teleprompter application for web and mobile devices. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Process

Information stored locally on your device:

  • Scripts you create, including titles, content, tags, highlights, and markers
  • Script revision history
  • App settings and onboarding status
  • Guest-mode status

This information is stored locally in your browser or app storage. We do not upload your scripts or settings to our own servers.

Optional account information:

  • If sign-in is enabled and you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, Firebase Authentication may provide us with account information such as your name, email address, and profile photo.
  • This information is used only to authenticate you and maintain your signed-in session.

Recordings you create:

  • When you use Record Mode, the app can access your camera and microphone to preview and record video with audio.
  • Recordings are created locally on your device or in your browser session and remain under your control.
  • You choose whether to save, share, or discard a recording.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create, edit, display, export, and restore your scripts
  • To save your app preferences locally on your device
  • To let you record video with camera and microphone access when you explicitly use Record Mode
  • To support optional sign-in through Firebase Authentication when enabled
  • To respond to support or deletion requests you send to us

3. Permissions and Sensitive Data

Camera and microphone: The app requests camera and microphone access only for features you initiate, such as Record Mode. Those inputs are used so you can preview and record locally in the app.

Voice control: If you enable voice control, the app uses the browser or device speech-recognition capability to detect spoken commands such as "play" or "pause." Depending on the browser, operating system, or device, speech processing may be handled by the platform provider and may not be performed entirely on-device. We do not use voice input for advertising or profiling.

Files and sharing: If you export a backup or save/share a recording, the app uses device or browser file features only to complete the action you requested.

4. Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal information. We only rely on third-party services that are necessary for optional app features:

  • Firebase Authentication for optional sign-in. See Firebase Privacy and Security.
  • Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In when you choose those providers through Firebase Authentication.
  • Browser or operating system speech-recognition services when you enable voice control.

5. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not upload your script content to our own servers.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs in the current version of the app.
  • We do not sell personal information to data brokers or other third parties.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local scripts, settings, and backups: remain on your device until you delete them, clear app/browser storage, or uninstall the app.
  • Recordings: remain only as long as you keep them in your device storage, browser downloads, or apps you share them with.
  • Account information: if sign-in is enabled and used, your authentication data is retained by the relevant identity provider and Firebase according to their policies until the account is deleted.

To request deletion of account-related data controlled by us, contact us using the email address below.

7. Security

We design the app to minimize data collection by keeping your scripts and settings on your device whenever possible. When third-party services such as Firebase Authentication are used, their security practices also apply.

8. Children's Privacy

Cuevora is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we will update the effective date on this page.
